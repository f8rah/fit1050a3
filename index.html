Arrows Performance Engineering specialises in road and track automotive engineering

At Arrows Performance Engineering, we specialise in supplying VASS services (Vehicle Assessment Signatory Scheme) for your Personal Import Vehicle, Pre-1989 Imported Vehicle, Individually Constructed Vehicle for cars, motorbikes, utilities and motorhomes. We help in all aspects of getting your vehicle on the road from Compliance certification, Road worthy certificate and final registration. We have helped people comply their cars from the UK, Singapore, Hong Kong, Ireland, and USA classics, sports cars and muscle cars. We also comply personal import and Pre-89 motorbikes.

Performance engineering is our passion, from engine tuning to full track setup. Speak to our team with your requirements.
